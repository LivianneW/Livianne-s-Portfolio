About Me

Hi, I’m Livianne! I am born and raised in Los Angeles, CA but I currently study cognitive science at UC San Diego. I love creating designs that feel natural and easy to use. I first discovered Figma back in community college and since then, I’ve been learning to bring my ideas to life. When I’m not building projects, you can usually find me experimenting in the kitchen (sometimes the designs comes out cleaner than the cookies).

How Ideas Come Alive

Whether through music, being in the kitchen, or designing, creativity has always been how I express myself. No matter the medium, I just really enjoy the process of trying out new ideas, tweaking them, and watching them come to life in ways that feel both meaningful and enjoyable.
