Spoonify

Simplifying your cooking journey, one recipe at a time

Overview
Spoonify is a recipe app designed to make meal prep simple, fun, and interactive. Its intuitive interface helps users easily explore a variety of recipes, from desserts to quick one-pan meals. Daily challenges add a social twist, letting users compete with friends to build streaks, while the custom recipe feature encourages sharing your own culinary creations and earning spoons as rewards
The Problem
As a home cooking enthusiast, I often struggled to find the right recipe that matches the ingredients I have on hand. I’d jump between YouTube, Google, and various apps, wasting precious time that could be spent actually cooking. After chatting with friends and family, I discovered that this frustration is a common one. Spoonify aims to solve this by streamlining recipe discovery in one easy-to-use platform
Process
I researched recipe apps, interviewed users, and created wireframes and prototypes in Figma.
Outcome
A clean, easy-to-use app with fun features like daily challenges and recipe sharing, helping users spend less time searching and more time cooking
