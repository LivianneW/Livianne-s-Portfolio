Coffees, smoothies, & teas

Overview
This project reimagined the Tastea website to better align with the brand identity seen on their social media. As a frequent customer, I noticed that the existing site felt outdated and didn’t reflect their refreshed style, so I set out to create a design that captures the brand’s energy and visual language.
The Problem
Tastea’s website experience was slowed by inconsistent item displays, unclear pricing, excessive scrolling, and a UI that no longer matched the brand’s identity. The goal was to reduce friction and make browsing faster, clearer, and more engaging.
Process
Reviewed the current site, gathered user feedback, and audited content to spot inconsistencies. Designed wireframes in Figma and prototyped in Feathery to streamline browsing and align visuals with the brand.
Outcome
A cleaner, faster site concept with consistent imagery, clear pricing, and a vibrant style that matches Tastea’s social media presence.
