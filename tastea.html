Tastea

Coffees, smoothies, & teas

Overview
This project reimagined the Tastea website to better align with the brand identity seen on their social media. As a frequent customer, I noticed that the existing site felt outdated and didn’t quite reflect their refreshed style, so I wanted to create a design that really reflects the brand’s personality.
The Problem
Tastea’s website experience was slowed by inconsistent item displays, unclear pricing, excessive scrolling, and a UI that no longer matched the brand’s identity. The goal was to reduce inconsistencies and make browsing faster, clearer, and more engaging.
Process
I reviewed the current site, gathered user feedback, and evaluated content to detect inconsistencies. Designed wireframes and prototyped in Figma to streamline browsing and align visuals with the brand.
Outcome
A cleaner, faster site concept with consistent imagery, clear pricing, and a vibrant style that matches Tastea’s social media presence.
