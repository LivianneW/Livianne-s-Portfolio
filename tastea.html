Resource Bridge 2025

Helping unhoused communities find essential resources through accessible design

Overview
This project focused on designing a digital kiosk that helps to connect unhoused individuals with local resources like shelters, meals, and medical care. The team emphasized simplicity, visibility, and self-agency in the user experience.
The Problem
Unhoused individuals often lack access to up-to-date and easy-to-use information about local services. Traditional resource lists are often times outdated, inaccessible, or overwhelming.
Process
We conducted interviews, observed how people interacted with public services, and wireframed a minimal, easy-to-use interface.
Outcome
A kiosk interface that lets users quickly filter for food, shelter, or assistance using large buttons, simple icons, and a map view.
